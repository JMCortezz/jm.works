About Me

I’m John Merwin M. Cortez, I’m from the Philippines, and I'm a self-taught web designer with almost a year of experience creating my personal projects and designs. I have strengths in visual design and making websites attractive to potential customers and users.

I intend to work on projects for local and international businesses and entrepreneurs. As a designer, my role is to listen to the client and create solutions that will satisfy them. I'm an aggressive learner who keeps up with design trends.

My Skills
Wireframing

I create a mid-fidelity wireframe to envision the primary features, functionalities, and content to help me enhance and adjust other website features that will satisfy the client.

Visual Design

I create designs that are always up to date and improve how the website will look, Knowing the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Bootstrap helps me a lot when I design websites.

UI/UX Design

I create user-friendly interfaces and easy-to-navigate designs that will help the users easily understand the interface so they can find all of its features easily without any hassle.

Web Design

Social Media AD Designs

-how i work

My Workflow
Briefing

First, I ask for as much information about the project as possible, such as the goals, details and concerns.

Researching

Second is researching, this step usually requires market research as well as competitive and visual comparisons.

Prototyping

Based on the research and analysis, I design the structures for the project and explain their functionalities to the client. 

Design

I select references to create unique and efficient designs. I evaluate many choices for visual solutions and choose fonts, colors, icons, and other graphic features. I present 2-3 pages and produce all additional pages after the design is approved.

Adaptation

I create adaptive versions for various electronic devices such as tablets and smartphone.

Finalization

I produce a Figma file, including a fully developed project, for transfer to the client.
