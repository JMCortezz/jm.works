ABOUT ME

I'm John Merwin M. Cortez, a self-taught web designer from the Philippines. With nearly a year of experience, I have strength in creating visually appealing websites that engage potential customers and users.

I intend to work on projects for local and international businesses and entrepreneurs. As a designer, my role is to listen to the client and create solutions that will satisfy them. I'm an aggressive learner who keeps up with design trends.

MY WORKFLOW
Briefing

I start by asking for lots of info about the project: goals, details, and concerns.

Researching

Researching follows, involving market analysis, competitive review, and visual comparisons for informed decision-making.

Prototyping

I use research to design project structures and explain how they work to clients.

Design

I pick styles, make designs, pick fonts/colors/icons, present drafts, and finalize approved designs.

Adaptation

I create adaptive versions for various electronic devices such as tablets and smartphone.

Finalization

I produce a Figma file, including a fully developed project, for transfer to the client.
